Overview

Geography: Kashmir is a region located in the northern part of the Indian subcontinent, nestled in the western Himalayas. It is divided between India, Pakistan, and China-administered territories. The Indian-administered part, known as Jammu and Kashmir, is further divided into three regions: Jammu, the Kashmir Valley, and Ladakh.


Natural Beauty: Classic Kashmir is renowned for its stunning landscapes, including lush green valleys, snow-capped mountains, pristine lakes, and meandering rivers. The iconic Dal Lake in Srinagar, surrounded by houseboats and shikaras (traditional wooden boats), is a symbol of the region's natural beauty.


Tourist Attractions: Kashmir is home to several tourist attractions, including: Mughal Gardens: These beautifully manicured gardens, such as Shalimar Bagh, Nishat Bagh, and Chashme Shahi, were built by the Mughal emperors and are known for their terraced lawns, fountains, and floral designs. Gulmarg: A popular hill station known for its lush meadows, skiing slopes, and the Gulmarg Gondola, one of the highest cable cars in the world. Pahalgam: A picturesque town surrounded by pine forests and meadows, serving as a base for treks and excursions to nearby attractions like Betaab Valley and Aru Valley.


Sonamarg: Known as the "Meadow of Gold," Sonamarg offers breathtaking views of glaciers, alpine lakes, and snow-capped peaks, making it a popular destination for trekking and camping. Cultural Heritage: Classic Kashmir has a rich cultural heritage influenced by its diverse population, including Kashmiri Muslims, Kashmiri Pandits, and other ethnic groups. The region is known for its traditional handicrafts such as pashmina shawls, Kashmiri carpets, papier-mâché items, and wood carvings. Cuisine: Kashmiri cuisine is known for its rich and aromatic flavors, with signature dishes like Rogan Josh (a spicy lamb curry),Dum Aloo (potatoes cooked in a spicy gravy), and Kahwa (a traditional green tea infused with spices and saffron).


The Classic Kashmir trip promises an unforgettable journey filled with wonder and discovery.
