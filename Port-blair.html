Overview

A visit to Port Blair is not just a journey to a breath-taking array of pristine beaches, rich history, and vibrant coral reefs, but it is also a step towards experiencing the heart of the Andaman Islands. When considering a visit to this tropical paradise, opting for a Port Blair tour package can streamline your trip, making it convenient and more enjoyable. The capital city of the Andamans offers a multitude of attractions, from the historic Cellular Jail to panoramic island views and incredible underwater adventures.
Securing a tour package to Port Blair often includes a variety of offerings such as hotel stays, guided tours, and meals. It may also feature activities like snorkelling, scuba diving, and island hopping. For travellers, Port Blair packages provide a structured plan for soaking up the sun on famous beaches like Corbyn’s Cove or discovering the unique marine life. In addition to the essential inclusions, these packages are crafted to cater to different interests and preferences, ensuring that your Andaman adventure is not just a trip but an experience tailored just for you.

Itinerary Enquire Now


Day 1: Arrival in Port Blair (Via Plane)

Upon arrival at the Airport, you'll be taken to the hotel.

After check-in, you can relax or visit Corbyn Cove Beach for scenic views. Later, you'll be taken for a visit to the Cellular Jail.

Enjoy a fantastic light & sound show before being driven to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner


DAY 2: Arrival in Havelock Island | Radhanagar Beach Tour

  • After checking out, you'll be taken to the Phoenix Bay Harbour for a ferry to Havelock Island.

  • Upon arrival and after checking in, visit Radhanagar Beach popular for its crystal clear waters and white sandy shores. Later, you'll be taken back to the hotel for an overnight stay.

  • Meals: Lunch &Dinner


DAY 3:Excursion to Elephant Beach

  • You will be taken to the jetty point for a speedboat ride to Elephant Beach.

  • On the way, enjoy water sports at Neemo Reef, on your own. Upon reaching, enjoy the scenic views.

  • Later, you will be taken back to the jetty point via speedboat, followed by a transfer to the hotel for an overnight stay.

  • Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner


DAY 4: Excursion to Kalapathar Beach | Transfer to Neil Island

  • Get driven to Kalapathar Beach. After reaching, walk around and admire the beautiful scenery.

  • Later, you will be taken back to the hotel for check out, followed by a transfer to the jetty point for a ferry ride to Neil Island.

  • Upon reaching, you will be taken to the hotel for an overnight stay.

  • Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner


DAY 5: Transfer to Port Blair | Enroute Sightseeing in Neil Island

  • After check out, get transferred to Port Blair. On the way, explore Neil Island, and visit Laxmanpur Beach, Sitapur Beach, and Bharatpur Beach.

  • You can also choose to enjoy water activities before being taken to Port Blair on a ferry, followed by a transfer to the hotel for an overnight stay.

  • Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner


DAY 6: Ross and North Bay Island Tour

  • Get ready for a ferry ride to Ross and North Bay Islands. On arrival at North Bay Island, enjoy various water activities like snorkelling, glass-bottom ride, scuba diving and more.

  • Later, witness the ruins of the Penal Colony in Ross Island and get transferred to your hotel for an overnight stay.

  • Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner


DAY 7:Departure Day

  • Post-checkout, get transferred to Veer Savarkar International Airport, marking the end of this trip.
