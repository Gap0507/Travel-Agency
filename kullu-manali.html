Overview

When someone mentions Kullu-Manali, visions of lush green valleys, snow-capped peaks, and meandering rivers immediately spring to mind. This region is not just a destination; it's a dreamy escape into nature's embrace, where every corner tells a story of serene beauty and adventure waiting to unfold.


From the moment you set foot in Kullu-Manali, you'll be enchanted by the refreshing mountain breeze and the picturesque vistas that greet you at every turn. This journey offers an opportunity to immerse yourself in the enchanting beauty of the Himalayas, with snow-capped mountains, glistening rivers, and verdant forests painting a mesmerizing backdrop.


But a trip to Kullu-Manali is not just about scenic landscapes; it's also a gateway to thrilling adventures and cultural experiences. Explore ancient temples nestled amidst towering peaks, stroll through vibrant markets brimming with local handicrafts, and savor the delectable flavors of Himachali cuisine.


Whether you're an avid adventurer seeking adrenaline-pumping activities like river rafting and paragliding or a nature lover craving tranquil moments amidst pristine wilderness, the Kullu-Manali trip promises an unforgettable journey filled with wonder and discovery.

Itinerary Enquire Now


Day 1: Arrival in Kullu-Manali

  • Arrive in Kullu-Manali and check into your hotel.

  • Spend the day exploring the local area and acclimatizing to the altitude.

  • Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner


Day 2: Rohtang Pass Excursion

  • Visit Rohtang Pass, one of the highest mountain passes in the world.

  • Enjoy snow activities like skiing and snowboarding (subject to weather conditions).

  • Return to Manali in the evening.


Day 3: Local Sightseeing in Manali

  • Visit Hadimba Devi Temple, Manu Temple, and Vashisht Temple.

  • Explore the Manali market for shopping.

  • Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner


Day 4: Solang Valley Adventure

  • Enjoy adventure activities like paragliding, zorbing, and ropeway rides.

  • Explore the beautiful landscapes of Solang Valley.

  • Return to Manali in the evening.


Day 5: Manikaran Excursion

  • Embark on a day trip to Manikaran, known for its hot springs and religious significance.

  • Visit the Gurudwara Manikaran Sahib and take a dip in the hot springs.

  • Explore the local market and try the famous langar (community kitchen) at the Gurudwara.

  • Return to Manali in the evening.

  • Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner

Day 6: Adventure Activities at Beas River

  • Enjoy thrilling adventure activities like river rafting and river crossing at the Beas River.

  • Experience the adrenaline rush as you navigate through the rapids and currents of the river.

  • Opt for other activities like trekking or camping along the riverbanks.

  • Return to Manali in the evening.

  • Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner

Day 7: Departure from Kullu-Manali

  • Check out from the hotel in Manali.

  • Depart for your hometown with beautiful memories of your Kullu-Manali trip.

  • Meals: Breakfast or Lunch (depending on departure time)
