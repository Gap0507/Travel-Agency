Overview

When someone mentions Leh, images of rugged mountains, pristine lakes, and the ancient monasteries of Ladakh come to mind. But Leh is more than just a picturesque destination; it's a paradise for adventure enthusiasts and nature lovers alike. Imagine riding through some of the highest motorable passes in the world, surrounded by breathtaking landscapes that seem straight out of a painting. This biking expedition offers an opportunity to experience the raw beauty of the Himalayas like never before.


From the moment you arrive in Leh, you'll be greeted by the crisp mountain air and panoramic views that stretch as far as the eye can see. As you embark on this thrilling journey, you'll traverse through dramatic terrain, crossing rivers, and navigating through winding roads that lead to hidden gems tucked away in the heart of the Himalayas.


But the Leh biking trip is not just about adrenaline-pumping adventures; it's also a chance to immerse yourself in the rich culture and traditions of Ladakh. Visit ancient monasteries that echo with the chants of Buddhist monks, explore charming villages where time seems to stand still, and indulge in the warm hospitality of the locals.


Whether you're an experienced rider seeking the ultimate biking challenge or a nature enthusiast looking to explore one of the most remote regions of the world, the Leh biking trip promises an unforgettable experience that will leave you with memories to last a lifetime.

Itinerary Enquire Now


Day 1: Arrival in Leh

  • Arrive in Leh and check into your hotel. Spend the day acclimatizing to the high altitude.

  • Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner


Day 2: Leh Local Sightseeing

  • Visit Shey Palace, Thiksey Monastery, and Hemis Monastery.

  • Explore Leh Market and Shanti Stupa.


Day 3: Leh to Nubra Valley

  • Ride through Khardung La, the highest motorable pass in the world.

  • Visit Diskit Monastery and explore the sand dunes of Hunder.

  • Stay overnight in Nubra Valley.


Day 4: Nubra Valley to Pangong Lake

  • Ride to Pangong Lake via Shyok Valley.

  • Enjoy the mesmerizing views of the lake and its changing colors.

  • Camp by the lakeside.


Day 5: Pangong Lake to Leh

  • Ride back to Leh.

  • Visit the monasteries of Chemrey and Thiksey on the way back.

  • Explore Leh in the evening.


Day 6: Leh to Tso Moriri

  • Ride to Tso Moriri Lake via Chumathang.

  • Enjoy the serene beauty of the lake and its surroundings.

  • Camp by the lakeside.


Day 7: Tso Moriri to Leh

  • Ride back to Leh.

  • Visit the village of Karzok and its monastery.

  • Relax in Leh in the evening.


Day 8: Leh to Khardung La

  • Ride to Khardung La, the highest motorable pass.

  • Take in the panoramic views from the pass.

  • Return to Leh and explore the local markets.


Day 9: Departure from Leh

  • Check out from the hotel.

  • Departure for Hometown With beautiful Memories
