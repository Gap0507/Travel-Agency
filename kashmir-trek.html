Overview

Welcome to the breathtaking world of Kashmir Trek, where nature's splendor meets the adventurous spirit. Nestled in the heart of the Himalayas, the Kashmir region offers some of the most awe-inspiring landscapes and thrilling trekking opportunities in the world. Whether you're a seasoned trekker or an adventurous soul seeking a new challenge, the Kashmir Trek promises an unforgettable expedition filled with mesmerizing vistas, cultural encounters, and unforgettable experiences.

The Kashmir Trek is a remarkable journey that takes you through the pristine beauty of the Kashmir Valley, offering a unique blend of natural wonders and cultural immersion. With its snow-capped peaks, lush meadows, dense forests, and sparkling rivers, this region is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts and nature lovers alike.

Embark on the adventure of a lifetime with the Kashmir Trek and discover the unparalleled beauty and charm of this enchanting region. Whether you're seeking a thrilling outdoor escapade or a soulful journey amidst nature's wonders, the Kashmir Trek promises an experience that will stay with you long after the journey ends. Join us as we explore the hidden gems and timeless landscapes of Kashmir,make memories that will last a lifetime.

DAY 1 : ARRIVAL AT SRINAGAR AND DRIVE TO SONMARG

  • 90 Km drive. Explore Srinagar at your own and aftrnoon we will take you from Srinagar to Sonamarg, where the basecamp for the trek is situated

  • Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner


Day 2: Start From Sonmarg To Nichnai Via Shekdur

  • Sonmarg (7,800 ft) to Nichnai (11,500 ft) via Shekdur Trekking distance of 11Km Trekking time around 8 hours

  • You will be starting your trek on Day 2 with a steep ascent towards the meadow of Shekdur, where the team will have a break before heading towards Nichnai.

  • The last stretch of today’s trek is a riverside trail through a valley with snow-clad peaks on both sides. The river valley marks the beginning of Nichnai and further downstream, there will be plenty of flat green meadows to put up our tents for the night.

  • Meals: Lunch &Dinner


Trek Nichnai to Vishnusar Lake via Nichnai Pass

  • Trek distance: 13.5 km | Trek Duration: 7 hours | Altitude gain: 11,607 ft to 12,011 ft via 13,229 ft

  • Trek type: Moderate. 1.5 hours of gradual ascent followed by an hour-long climb to the pass. A steep descent from the pass for about an hour easing off into a flat walk.

  • Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner


Day 4 :- Rest day


DAY 5 :- Trek from Vishnusar to Gadsar via Gadsar Pass

  • Trek distance: 16 km | Trek Duration: 7.5 hours | Altitude gain and loss: 12,011 ft to 10,706 ft via 13,715 ft

  • Trek type: Moderate difficult. 1.5-2 hours of steep ascent followed by 1 hour of steep descent, easing off into level walk.

  • Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner


DAY 6 :- Trek from Gadsar to Satsar

  • Trek distance: 11.5 km | Trek Duration: 4.5 hours | Altitude gain: 10,706 ft to 11,985 ft

  • Trek type: Moderate. 1.5 hours of steep ascent followed by a level walk.

  • Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner


DAY 7 :- Trek from Satsar to Gangabal via Zaj Pass

  • Trek distance: 9 km | Trek Duration: 6 hours | Altitude loss: 11,985 ft to 11,486 ft via 13,276 ft

  • Trek type: Difficult. 30 minutes each of gradual ascent followed by steep ascent for about 45 minutes and then a steep descent. Similarly ascending and descending trail all through.

  • Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner


DAY 8 :- Trek from Gangabal to Naranag. Drive to Srinagar

  • Trek distance: 13 km | Trek Duration: 6 hours | Altitude loss: 11,486 ft to 7,800 ft Drive Distance: 2 hours | Drive Duration: 50 km

  • Trek type: Moderate. A mix of ascents and descents for 6 km followed by a very steep descent all the way down. You should reach Srinagar by 6 pm.

  • Please note: The distance between campsites may vary by 100 meters depending the weather conditions and the route you take. The altitude may also vary by 100 feet for similar reasons.

  • Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner


Day 9 :- Explore Dal lake and Leave for Hometown With Beautiful memories

  • After breakfast we are enjoying Sikara ride and Explore Dal lake. and after that departure for Hometown With beautiful Memories.
