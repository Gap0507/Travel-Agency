Overview

Rameshwaram is located on a beautiful island in the South Indian state of Tamil Nadu. It is separated by a small Pamban channel from Sri Lanka. According to Hindu mythology, this is the place where Lord Rama created a bridge across the sea to Sri Lanka.

Rameshwaram has the first sea bridge connecting the town of Mandapam with Pamban Island, and Rameswaram. Abdandoned town of Dhanushkodi is also located on Pamban Island which was a bustling town until it got destroyed in 1964 by cyclone.

Renowned for its magnificent prakaras with massive sculptured pillars on either side, The Ramanathaswamy Temple houses the longest corridor in the world. Agniteertham is famous for its sacred waters and Pilgrims perform poojas in honour of their ancestors at this seashore. The five-faced Hanuman Temple holds the floating stone which was used to build the bridge between India and Sri Lanka.

Day 1: Arrival in Madurai (Via Train)

  • Upon arrival in Madurai, you will get transferred to your hotel.

  • After freshening up, visit Meenakshi Temple, the oldest yet significant attraction among Indians. Later, transfer back to your hotel and enjoy the rest of the day at leisure.

  • Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner


DAY 2: Madurai to Kodaikanal (VIA BUS)

  • Check out from the hotel and get transferred to Kodaikanal.

  • Check-in and spend the day at leisure.

  • You can visit Kodaikanal Lake, one of the most beautiful and visited places. Later, transfer to your hotel & spend the day at your own pace.

  • Meals: Lunch &Dinner


DAY 3:Kodaikanal Sightseeing Tour

  • Begin your day with a fun-filled sightseeing tour of Kodaikanal.

  • Visit beautiful and amazing places like Pillar Rock, Bear Shola Fall, and many more. After exploring all these attractions, transfer back to your hotel for an overnight stay.

  • Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner


DAY 4: Arrival in Rameshwaram

  • Check-out from your hotel at Kodaikanal and transfer to Rameswaram.

  • Check-in and spend the day at leisure. Overnight stay at hotel.

  • Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner


DAY 5: Rameshwaram Sightseeing Tour

  • Go on a sightseeing tour of Rameswaram and explore attractions such as Ramanathaswamy Temple and Gandhamadhana Parvatham. Later, transfer back to your hotel for an overnight stay.

  • Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner


DAY 6: Arrival in Kanyakumari

  • Get transferred to the hotel in Kanyakumari from Rameswaram. Check-in and spend your day at leisure.

  • You can visit popular attractions like Thirparappu Falls, Kanyakumari Beach, and many more. Overnight stay at the hotel.

  • Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner


DAY 7: Kanyakumari to Kovalam and Departure

  • Head to Kovalam and Madurai for your onward journey. En route explore attractions like Vivekananda Rock Memorial, Gandhi Memorial Museum, Thiruvalluvar Statue, and Sri Kumari Amman Temple.

  • Transfer to your hotel in Kovalam for an overnight stay.

  • After exploring these gems, pack your bags with memories and get transferred to Trivandrum Airport for your onward journey.

  • Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
