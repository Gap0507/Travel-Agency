Overview

"Chadar" refers to the sheet of ice that from over the Zanskar River as it transforms it self from a rapid flowing river into a white blanket of ice during winter. It becomes a frozen spectacle of glass ice ranging from a bluish tinge to golden yellow that is seen during the few hours that sunlight reaches directly into the gorge to the milky whitish on a moonlit night - as said a trek journey like no other. Chadar connects villages in the Zanskar valley(deep in the mountains)with Chilling(on the road to Leh)along the frozen Zanskar River. This rout has been used for centuries for trade and transportation, and is most reliable in February when the ice is most stable.

  • Visit Shey Palace, Thiksey Monastery, and Hemis Monastery.

Day 03: Leh to Chilling and Trek to Tilad Do

  • Drive from Leh to Chilling and start trekking to Tilad Do.

  • Set up camp at Tilad Do and spend the night.

  • Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner


Day 04: Tilad Do to Shingra Koma

  • Continue trekking from Tilad Do to Shingra Koma.

  • Set up camp at Shingra Koma and spend the night.

  • Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner


Day 05: Shingra Koma to Tibb Cave

  • Trek from Shingra Koma to Tibb Cave.

  • Set up camp at Tibb Cave and spend the night.

  • Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner


Day 06: Tibb Cave to Naerak Camp

  • Trek from Tibb Cave to Naerak Camp.

  • Explore the stunning frozen waterfalls at Naerak.

  • Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner


Day 07: Naerak to Tibb Cave

  • Trek back from Naerak Camp to Tibb Cave.

  • Set up camp at Tibb Cave and spend the night.

  • Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner


Day 08: Tibb Cave to Shingra Koma

  • Trek from Tibb Cave to Shingra Koma.

  • Set up camp at Shingra Koma and spend the night.

  • Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner


Day 09: Shingra Koma to Tilad Do and Drive to Leh
