Popular Destinations

UDAIPUR

Explore Udaipur, a gem in the heart of Rajasthan renowned for its shimmering lakes, opulent palaces, and vibrant culture. Lose yourself in the grandeur of the City Palace, stroll through the bustling markets of the old city, and cruise along the tranquil waters of Lake Pichola. With its rich history, breathtaking vistas, and warm hospitality, Udaipur promises an unforgettable journey into the royal heritage of India.

DELHI

Explore Delhi, a vibrant metropolis blending history and modernity. Explore iconic landmarks like the Red Fort,Humayun's Tomb, India Gate, Raj Ghat, Qutub Minar and Jama Masjid, and immerse yourself in the city's diverse culture and cuisine. From bustling bazaars to serene parks, witness the hustle and bustle of Chandni Chowk and Delhi offers a captivating blend of tradition and contemporary life, making it a must-visit destination for every traveler.

KOLKATA

Explore Kolkata, a city brimming with history, culture, and charm. Marvel at the majestic Victoria Memorial, stroll along the iconic Howrah Bridge, and savor delectable street food delights. Delve into the city's rich literary heritage at the College Street Book Market, or unwind amidst the serene beauty of the Maidan. With its vibrant festivals and warm hospitality, Kolkata invites you to discover its unique soul at every turn.

MUMBAI

Explore Mumbai, the bustling metropolis that never sleeps. From the iconic Gateway of India to the vibrant markets of Colaba Causeway, experience the eclectic blend of cultures, cuisines, and lifestyles. Dive into the pulsating energy of Bollywood, stroll along the scenic Marine Drive, or indulge in a culinary adventure at the street food stalls. With its endless opportunities, Mumbai promises an unforgettable journey.

GOA

Explore Goa, India's premier beach destination renowned for its sun-kissed shores, vibrant nightlife, and Portuguese heritage. Relax on the golden sands of Anjuna Beach, party till dawn at the legendary clubs of Baga, and savor the flavors of authentic Goan cuisine. Discover ancient churches, bustling markets, and hidden coves as you immerse yourself in the laid-back charm of this coastal paradise.

Jaipur

Explore Jaipur, the vibrant capital of Rajasthan, where rich history and cultural heritage come alive. Marvel at the majestic Amber Fort, stroll through the bustling bazaars of the Pink City, and immerse yourself in the royal splendor of the City Palace. From admiring the intricate architecture of Hawa Mahal to experiencing traditional Rajasthani cuisine.Jaipur promises an unforgettable journey into the heart of Rajasthan's royal legacy.
